Macaroni

Description

This recipe is intended to teach those willing how to make creamy marcaroni.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • black pepper
  • garlic
  • onion
  • basil
  • Cheese
  • 2% Milk
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Mix black pepper, garlic, onion, basil, parsley, cheese, and milk together.
  2. Boil pasta until no longer chewy.
  3. Drain pot and add mixture to pasta.
  4. Stir for 10 minutes on low heat.
  5. Enjoy!
