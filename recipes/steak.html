Steak

Description

This recipe is intended to teach those willing how to make a nice medium rare steak by grill.

  • Ribeye Steak
  • black pepper
  • garlic
  • onion
  • basil
  • grape seed oil

Steps

  1. Mix black pepper, garlic, onion, and basil together
  2. Apply to mixed seasoning to steak
  3. Put grapeseed oil in pan and put steak in after about 15 minutes of heating
  4. Grill steak for 25 minutes and once finished leave steak to sit for 5 minutes.
  5. Enjoy!
