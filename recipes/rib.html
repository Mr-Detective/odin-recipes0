Rib

Description

This recipe is intended to teach those willing how to make nice tender pork ribs by oven.

Ingredients

  • Pork ribs
  • black pepper
  • garlic
  • onion
  • basil
  • grape seed oil

Steps

  1. Mix black pepper, garlic, onion, basil, and grapeseed oil together
  2. Lather mixed seasoning on ribs
  3. Put paper sheet on pan and preheat oven to 375 degrees fahrenheit.
  4. Bake Ribs for 25 minutes and once finished leave steak to sit for 5 minutes.
  5. Enjoy!
